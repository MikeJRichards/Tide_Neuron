Mission Statement

“At TideNeuron, our mission is to protect smaller investors from the negative impacts of whales and sharks, and to empower them with the ability to influence and manipulate market cycles. We also aim to enable investors to access high 8-year ICP staking rewards by staking TDN, thereby growing their ICP holdings. By leveraging innovative staking and reward mechanisms, we ensure that every investor, regardless of their stake size, can participate in market movements on an equal footing, fostering a fairer and more balanced market environment.”
